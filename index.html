Quick Start: How to Use DACUM Lite

  1. 1Enter the Occupation Title and Job Title in the Chart Info tab.
  2. 2Add a Scope of Work to define the occupational boundary.
  3. 3Go to Duties & Tasks — each new duty starts with one task automatically.
  4. 4Use ➕ Add Task inside each duty. Duties are labelled A, B, C… and tasks A1, A2, B1… automatically.
  5. 5Drag & drop any task or duty to reorder — labels update instantly.
  6. 6Switch to 🃏 Card View or 🧱 Wall View for different display modes.
  7. 7Fill in Additional Info (Knowledge, Skills, Tools, Trends…) as needed.
  8. 8Fill in Workshop Panel (Facilitators, Observers, Panel Members) in Chart Info.
  9. 9Export as PDF or Word document from the top toolbar.
  10. 10Use 📸 Snapshots to save named versions before major edits.
