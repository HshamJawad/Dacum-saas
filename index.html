Quick Start: How to Use DACUM Lite

  1. 1 Enter the Occupation Title and Job Title in the Chart Info tab.
  2. 2 Add a Scope of Work description to define the occupational boundary.
  3. 3 Go to Duties & Tasks and add duties — each duty starts with one task automatically.
  4. 4 Use ➕ Add Task inside each duty to expand the task list. Duties are labelled A, B, C… and tasks A1, A2, B1… automatically.
  5. 5 Drag & drop any task or duty to reorder — labels update instantly.
  6. 6 Switch to 🃏 Card View for a horizontal layout or 🧱 Wall View for the classic DACUM workshop display.
  7. 7 Fill in Additional Info (Knowledge, Skills, Tools, Trends…) as needed.
  8. 8 Fill in Workshop Panel (Facilitators, Observers, Panel Members) in Chart Info.
  9. 9 Export as PDF or Word document from the top toolbar.
  10. 10 Use 📸 Snapshots to save named versions before major edits.
